Our Service Offerings

Services

At Vibron, we provide comprehensive electronic product development solutions, guiding projects from concept to production. With expertise in hardware design, firmware development, software development, and system integration, we collaborate with industry-leading partners for PCB assembly, manufacturing, and enclosure design to deliver high-quality, scalable, and cost-effective solutions.

System Architecture & Product Design

Seamless integration of hardware, firmware, and software for scalable and efficient systems.

  • System feasibility analysis and requirement mapping.
  • Embedded hardware-software co-design.
  • Scalable and modular architecture.

PCB Design & Hardware Development

We create optimized, high-performance PCB layouts that meet industry standards, ensuring efficiency and reliability.

  • High-speed and multi-layer PCB design.
  • Signal integrity and power management optimization.
  • DFM (Design for Manufacturing) and DFT (Design for Testability).

Component Sourcing & Supply Chain Management

We ensure reliable and cost-effective component sourcing to streamline production.

  • BOM optimization and lifecycle management.
  • Global component sourcing and vendor management.
  • Risk assessment for obsolescence and lead-time reduction.

Embedded Firmware Development

Our team develops bare-metal and RTOS-based firmware, along with IoT and cloud-based applications, ensuring seamless connectivity and security.

  • Embedded firmware development (C, C++, RTOS, Linux).
  • Protocol development (I2C, SPI, UART, CAN, BLE, Wi-Fi, LoRa).
  • Secure boot, OTA updates, and cybersecurity implementations.
  • API and cloud integration for IoT solutions.

User-Friendly Software Design & Development

We design robust and scalable software solutions tailored for embedded systems, and cloud applications.

  • Application development (Desktop, Mobile, and Web).
  • Embedded software and middleware development.
  • Cloud-based software and API development.
  • Database design, management, and optimization.
  • Automated data collection and real-time analytics.
  • Cybersecurity, encryption, and secure communication.

End-to-End Electronic Product Development & Manufacturing

Covering the entire process from design to mass production, ensuring seamless integration, high quality, and scalability.

  • From concept to production, ensuring seamless hardware, firmware, and software integration.
  • Rapid prototyping, validation, and testing for reliable performance.
  • Scalable manufacturing with DFM and quality assurance for mass production.
  • Ensuring cost-effective procurement and efficient production management.
